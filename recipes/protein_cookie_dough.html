Protein Cookie Dough Recipe

Photo by Brigitte Tohm from Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/photo/brown-cookies-189536/

A delicate delight

While it may not be cookie dough, it tastes good enough to be like one.

Ingredients:

  • 25g of Peanut Butter
  • 1 tbsp of Sugar-Free Syrup
  • 1 Scoop Vanilla Whey Protein Powder
  • 15 g of Mini Chocolate Chips

Steps:

  1. Toss your peanut butter into a bowl
  2. Toss in your sugar-free syrup and whey scoop
  3. Mix it all up
  4. Add mini chocolate chips, and mix again
  5. Enjoy!
