Yogurt Bowl Recipe

A yummy looking yogurt bowl. Credits

This sweet treat is bound to brighten up your day. That soft creamy texture is really to put you in a mood.

Ingredients:

  • 150 g of Vanilla Greek Yogurt
  • 50g of Protein Granola
  • 1 Medium Banana, sliced
  • 20g Mixed Berries
  • 1 scoop of protein powder of your choice (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Place your greek yogurt in a bowl
  2. (If you don't have protein powder, you can skip to step 3) Mix your protein scoop into the yogurt and mix!
  3. Toss in your protein Granola
  4. Top off with sliced bananas, and mixed berries
  5. Enjoy!
