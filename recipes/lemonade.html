Lemonade Recipe

Photo by DESIGNbyJA from Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/photo/lemon-juice-on-selective-focus-photography-2109099/

A refreshing drink

Perfect balance of sweet and tangy with that refreshing vibe to it.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 8 whole lemons
  • 4 cups of water
  • Ice Cubes (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Zest your lemon
  2. Boil 1 cup of water, and mix with your sugar
  3. Toss in lemon zest, and stir continously until sugar dissolves. This is your lemon-flavored syrup.
  4. Once dissolved, pour your syrup into a container, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  5. While you're waiting for your syrup to cool, slice and juice your lemons.
  6. After your syrup if cooled down, pour both your lemon juice and syrup into a pitcher.
  7. Toss in 3 cups of cold water, and your ice cubes. Stir well, and enjoy!
